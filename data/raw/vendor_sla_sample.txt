Vendor Alpha Service Level Agreement

1. Uptime Commitment
Vendor Alpha guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime for the hosted procurement platform.

2. Service Credits
If uptime falls below 99.9% but remains above 99.0%, the customer receives a 10% service credit.
If uptime falls below 99.0%, the customer receives a 25% service credit.

3. Response Time
Critical incidents must receive an initial response within 1 hour.
High priority incidents must receive an initial response within 4 hours.
Medium priority incidents must receive an initial response within 1 business day.

4. Data Retention
Vendor Alpha retains customer audit logs for 365 days.

5. Renewal Terms
The agreement renews automatically for 12 months unless canceled 30 days before renewal.
