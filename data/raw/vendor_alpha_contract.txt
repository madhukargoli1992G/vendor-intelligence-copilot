Vendor Alpha Contract Terms

1. Renewal
The contract automatically renews for another 12 month period unless cancelled 30 days prior.

2. Termination
Either party may terminate the agreement with 30 days written notice.

3. Liability
Vendor Alpha's liability is limited to the annual contract value.

4. Data Ownership
All customer data remains the property of the customer.

5. Compliance
Vendor Alpha complies with SOC2 Type II security standards.
